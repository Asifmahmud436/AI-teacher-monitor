# AI teacher monitor 
An AI app that analyzes the full lecture of a teacher. In third world countries there always remains a possibility that a teacher lazily passes time, scolds or behaves bad with his students or maybe fails in any other ways to fulfill his duties. This app uses AI to monitor the behaviours of the teachers and flags potential instructional and behavioural risks for human review
